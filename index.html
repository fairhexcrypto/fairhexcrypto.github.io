FairHEX

What is FairHEX?

FairHEX is a token built on top of HEX
FairHEX has a fixed supply of 21 million tokens
FairHEX rewards holders with HEX

How do I claim my free tokens ?

You stake HEX to claim free FairHEX
You will receive your free tokens instantly upon staking
When 5 million tokens are minted, the minting phase ends

Is the platform safe ?

FairHEX has no sacrifice phase, no admin keys, locked liquidity and token supply
Code is open source and verifiable on Etherscan
